Tell the court about your financial circumstances

Complete this form before your hearing, when the court asks you to, or on the day of your hearing. The court may decide to fine you.

If you do not tell the court about your financial circumstances, or you do not give reliable information, it could mean you are not fined the correct amount.

It is an offence to make a false statement or knowingly to fail to disclose material facts when providing the court with a statement of your assets and financial circumstances, following an official request.

Paying a fine

If the court orders that you pay a fine or other costs, you may be ordered to pay on the day. You should bring a method of payment with you.

If you cannot pay on the day, you can ask for time to pay. The court can set up a payment plan.
