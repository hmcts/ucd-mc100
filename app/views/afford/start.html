Tell the court about your financial circumstances

Complete this form before your hearing, when the court asks you to, or on the day of your hearing. The court may decide to fine you.

If the court fines you and you have not provided your financial circumstances, or you give inaccurate information, you may not fined the correct amount.

It is an offence to lie or not include relevant facts when providing the court with a statement of your financial circumstances.

Paying a fine

If the court orders that you pay a fine or other costs, you may be ordered to pay on the day. You should bring a method of payment with you.

If you cannot pay on the day, you can ask the court to set up a payment plan.

Before you start

You will be asked to give information including:

  • your National Insurance number
  • your case reference number - find this on any correspondance from the court
  • your employment
  • any benefits you receive
  • your income and spending
