What you need to continue

Before you continue with this service your need your case reference number or unique reference number (URN). This is so we can match you with the correct case.

Your case reference can be found on any official correspondence from the court or enforcement office, or on your hearing notice.

If you are unable to find any correspondence you can get a copy by calling the Courts and Tribunals Service Centre.
